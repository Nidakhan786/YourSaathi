Welcome to YourSaathi a Counselling Platform for all the Women who are trying to best heal from things they do not discuss.

What We Do

A Bot to listen to their problems and to help them book their appointment with a counsellor. Anonymous counselling Face to face counselling A Forum to discuss your problems with a group of people

Our Goal

A woman faces problems or challenges in her day to day life either socially or physically. By playing so many roles at the same time, be it as a mother, sister, daughter, wife and many more, she eventually loses her own identity or struggles to maintain it.Women often restricts themselves from disclosing their problems due to their family reputation. Therefore we came up with YourSaathi to emancipate women and to let her live her life with full dignity and choice.
